Elementary to High School Student Pathways: Arthur A. Benjamin Health Professions High (HPHS) 9th Grade Class (2015-16)

This diagram displays where the 28 9th grade students at HPHS in 2015-16 went to 8th grade (2014-15) and 6th grade (2012-13) within the Sacramento City Unified School District (SCUSD).

Move your cursor over the diagram to view the number of students in each pathway.

Note: 7th grade is not displayed because most students went to the same school for 7th and 8th grade. For some of the 28 students at HPHS, no school data was provided for 6th and/or 8th grades. These students are placed in the 'Out of District' categories as they must have entered from a school outside of SCUSD.

Data provided by Sacramento City Unified School District.
